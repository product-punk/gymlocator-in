About

Arjun has spent the last nine years on gym floors across Bangalore, Mumbai and Pune — first as a competitive powerlifter, then as a personal trainer, and now as Gymlocator's lead gym reviewer.

He believes the best gym is the one you'll actually show up to, and his reviews focus on the things that matter day to day: equipment quality, crowd levels at peak hours, hygiene, and whether the staff actually know their stuff. Every review is based on at least one full in-person session — never a press release.

"Consistency beats intensity. A good gym removes every excuse you have not to go."
Written by Arjun
